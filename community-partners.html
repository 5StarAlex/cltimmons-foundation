FAQ

Common partnership questions.

Is there a cost to become a Community Donation Partner?

No. Community Donation Partners help provide convenient donation drop-off access for approved foundation initiatives.

How often are donations picked up?

Pickups are scheduled based on donation volume and partner needs.

What items can we accept?

Clothing, school supplies, hygiene products, household essentials, and other approved donations.

Can businesses sponsor a scholarship?

Yes. The foundation can discuss scholarship, education, event, and community program sponsorship opportunities.

Can individuals volunteer?

Absolutely. Volunteers are an important part of the mission.

Ready to partner?

Whether you want to host a donation site, sponsor a program, volunteer, or explore another partnership opportunity, we would love to connect.
