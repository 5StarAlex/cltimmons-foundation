Cathy's Closet

Practical support, offered with dignity.

Cathy's Closet connects donated clothing, school supplies, hygiene products, and household essentials with neighbors and families who can use them most.

Host a Drop-Off Site

What We Collect

Everyday items can become meaningful support.

Donation needs will shift by season and community request. The foundation will share approved donation lists before each drive so partners and donors know what is most helpful.

Clothing

New or gently used seasonal clothing, coats, uniforms, and shoes.

School Supplies

Backpacks, notebooks, writing supplies, calculators, and classroom essentials.

Home & Hygiene

Personal care items, household basics, and approved family support items.
